Seamless Team Communication

Connect every role in your operation with integrated messaging, alerts, and notifications that keep everyone in sync.

Real-Time, Role-Based Messaging

Eliminate radio chatter and missed calls. Our secure, built-in messaging allows for direct, two-way communication between dispatchers, drivers, and managers. Send route updates, safety alerts, or general information instantly.

  • Direct one-to-one and group messaging
  • Message history logged for accountability
  • Reduces driver distraction and improves focus

Automated System Alerts

Stay ahead of issues with proactive notifications. OmniLink automatically alerts the relevant personnel about critical events, such as a vehicle maintenance issue reported on a DVIR, an upcoming certification expiry, or a significant route deviation.

  • Customizable alert triggers and recipients
  • Notifications delivered via platform and email
  • Ensures critical information is never missed

Broadcast Announcements

Quickly disseminate important information to your entire team or specific groups. Use broadcast announcements for weather warnings, policy updates, or company-wide news. Ensure consistent messaging and keep everyone informed.

  • Send messages to all staff, all drivers, or custom groups
  • Track read-receipts to confirm messages are seen
  • Ideal for urgent updates and general announcements
