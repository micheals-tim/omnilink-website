Data-Driven Management

Gain a high-level view of your entire operation. Turn complex data into clear, actionable insights to drive efficiency and growth.

Comprehensive Dashboard

Your entire operation, summarized. The manager dashboard brings together key performance indicators (KPIs) from every module, giving you an instant overview of on-time performance, vehicle health, safety incidents, and budget adherence.

  • Track key metrics in real-time
  • Customize widgets to focus on what matters most
  • Drill down into specific modules for more detail

Customizable Reporting

Go beyond the dashboard with powerful reporting tools. Generate detailed reports on fuel usage, maintenance costs, driver performance, route efficiency, and more. Export data to share with stakeholders or for further analysis.

  • Schedule recurring reports to be sent automatically
  • Use pre-built templates or create your own custom reports
  • Visualize data with charts and graphs

User & Permissions Management

Maintain complete control over your system. The manager module allows you to easily add or remove users, define roles, and set granular permissions, ensuring team members can only access the information and tools relevant to their job.

  • Create custom roles with specific access rights
  • View audit logs of user activity
  • Integrate with Single Sign-On (SSO) providers
