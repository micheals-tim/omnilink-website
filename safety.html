A Proactive Approach to Safety

Build a culture of safety and compliance with integrated tools for incident reporting, driver certifications, and digital audits.

Centralized Incident Reporting

Empower any team member to log incidents the moment they happen. Capture critical details, attach photos or documents, and automatically notify the right people. Create a complete, auditable record for every event, from minor scrapes to major accidents.

  • Log incidents from desktop or mobile devices
  • Track incident status from report to resolution
  • Analyze trends to identify and mitigate risks

Compliance & Certification Tracking

Never miss an expiration date again. OmniLink tracks all driver licenses, medical certificates, and required training. Receive automated alerts for upcoming renewals to ensure your entire team remains compliant and road-ready.

  • Central digital repository for all documents
  • Automated alerts for pending expirations
  • Maintain a complete history of training and certifications

Digital Safety Audits

Conduct and store vehicle safety inspections, facility audits, and driver performance reviews digitally. Use customizable templates to fit your specific needs and maintain a searchable, secure record of all safety-related activities.

  • Create custom checklists for any type of audit
  • Schedule recurring safety tasks and inspections
  • Easily export data for regulatory reporting
