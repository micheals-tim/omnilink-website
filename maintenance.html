Proactive Vehicle Maintenance

Minimize downtime and extend the life of your vehicles with a fully integrated maintenance and repair workflow.

Automated Maintenance Scheduling

Move from reactive fixes to proactive care. Set up service reminders based on mileage, engine hours, or time intervals. OmniLink automatically notifies you when a vehicle is due for service, helping you prevent costly breakdowns.

  • Create custom service schedules for each vehicle type
  • Receive automated alerts for upcoming maintenance
  • Improve vehicle longevity and reliability

Digital Work Order Management

Streamline your entire repair process. Create, assign, and track digital work orders from start to finish. Technicians can see their assigned tasks, log parts used, and record time spent, giving you a clear view of your maintenance workflow.

  • Generate work orders from failed DVIR items
  • Track labor and parts costs for each job
  • Monitor work order status in real-time

Comprehensive Service History

Every inspection, service, and repair is automatically logged against the vehicle's profile. Access a complete, searchable service history for any vehicle in your fleet to identify recurring issues, track warranty information, and understand the total cost of ownership.

  • Full digital audit trail for every vehicle
  • Analyze maintenance costs and trends over time
  • Make informed decisions on vehicle replacement
