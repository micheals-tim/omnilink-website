Real-Time Operational Awareness

Make decisions based on what's happening now, not yesterday. OmniLink provides a live, unified view of your entire operation.

Live GPS Vehicle Tracking

Know the exact location of every vehicle in your fleet at all times. Our live map provides second-by-second updates, allowing you to monitor route progress, respond to incidents, and provide accurate ETAs to stakeholders.

  • High-frequency updates for precision tracking
  • View vehicle speed, direction, and status
  • Set up geofences to get alerts on entry/exit

Instant Status Updates

Data from every module flows into a unified stream of information. When a driver completes a DVIR, a dispatcher assigns a trip, or a technician finishes a work order, the status is updated instantly for everyone who needs to know.

  • Driver clock-in/out and route start/end times
  • Live updates from vehicle telematics (e.g., engine faults)
  • Breaks down information silos between departments

Historical Data Playback

Investigate incidents or review performance with a detailed "breadcrumb" trail of historical data. Replay any vehicle's route from a specific day to see its path, speed, and stops, providing invaluable context for safety and training.

  • Select any date and vehicle to review its history
  • Analyze speeding, harsh braking, or idling events
  • Export data for incident reports or performance reviews
