The Driver's Co-Pilot

Empower your drivers with the tools they need to be safe, efficient, and connected on every route.

Clear Turn-by-Turn Navigation

Eliminate guesswork and reduce driver stress. OmniLink provides clear, optimized turn-by-turn directions for every assigned route, delivered directly to the driver's mobile device. Ensure on-time performance and route adherence.

  • Live map view with clear stop sequences
  • Voice-guided directions for hands-free operation
  • Automatic updates for last-minute route changes

Effortless Digital Inspections (DVIR)

Replace cumbersome paper forms with intuitive digital checklists. Drivers can complete pre-trip and post-trip vehicle inspections quickly on their mobile device, ensuring compliance and instantly flagging issues for the maintenance team.

  • Customizable inspection checklists
  • Attach photos to document defects
  • Instant submission and maintenance notifications

Stay Connected & Informed

Direct, two-way communication between drivers and dispatch is built right in. Receive real-time alerts about traffic, delays, or route changes. Send quick status updates without needing to make a phone call.

  • Secure two-way messaging
  • Receive important broadcast alerts
  • Reduces distractions and improves focus
