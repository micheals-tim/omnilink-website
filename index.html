Core Modules

OmniLink provides dedicated tools for every member of your team, ensuring seamless communication and operational excellence.

Dispatcher

A powerful command center to monitor your entire operation in real-time, manage assignments, and communicate instantly.

Driver

Empower drivers with a dedicated mobile portal for turn-by-turn navigation, digital vehicle inspections (DVIR), and direct messaging.

Router

Build, manage, and optimize routes with drag-and-drop simplicity. Respond to real-time events and ensure maximum efficiency.

Safety

Centralize incident reporting, track driver certifications, and maintain a digital audit trail for all compliance documents.

Maintenance

Automate preventative maintenance schedules, manage work orders, and track parts inventory to minimize vehicle downtime.

Manager

Access a comprehensive dashboard with key performance indicators. Make data-driven decisions to improve your entire operation.

Platform-Wide Features

Communication

Reporting
